Controls

Player 1: Arrow keys to move, Space to shoot, B for smart bomb.
Player 2: W/A/S/D keys to move, F to shoot, G for smart bomb.
Player 3 & 4: Joins with Gamepad D-pad or Left Stick, Button A (0) to shoot, Button B (1) for smart bomb.

Move or press buttons to dynamically Hot-Join! Smart bomb destroys all viewport ghosts.

DANDY DUNGEON

A retro masterpiece, modernized in Haskell WebAssembly.

Player Score Health Keys Bombs
P1 0 100 0 0
Player 2: Press WASD/F/G to Join
Player 3: Connect Gamepad 1 & Press A to Join
Player 4: Connect Gamepad 2 & Press A to Join
This game requires a browser that supports the HTML5 canvas tag.

About

Experience the faithful Haskell WebAssembly port of the classic Atari 800 game Dandy Dungeon. Navigate treacherous levels, collect keys, and blast monsters in this updated HTML5 implementation.
